Cite

Cite#

If you use abTEM in your research, we kindly ask that you cite our main paper:

Jacob Madsen and Toma Susi. The abTEM code: transmission electron microscopy from first principles. Open Research Europe 1:24, 2021. doi:10.12688/openreseurope.13015.1.

You can download a .bib file of the reference here.

In addition, we recommend that you also cite the original works of any relevant methods and algorithms that your use relies on (e.g. PRISM, GPAW, or potential parametrizations; see bibliography).
